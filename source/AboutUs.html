About US

Capvital Advisory private
Limited

We, Unistone Capital Private Limited (‘Capvital’) are a SEBI registered Merchant Banker engaged in Investment Banking activities ranging from Lead Management, Fund Raising and Corporate Advisory services. 

Our umbrella of services includes IPOs, FPOs, Open Offers (takeovers), Rights Issues, Preferential Allotments/ QIPs, PIPE Deals, VC Funding, Fund Raising (both, listed and unlisted companies), Valuation Advisory and Debt Advisory.

In addition to these, our team, at their previous organizations have executed a range of advisory and large transactions for eminent clients such as Indigo Paints, Essar Group, Bikaji Foods, Sunteck Group, AU Small Finance Bank, Haldiram’s, Future Group, amongst others.

Catalyst Capital Partners Private Limited (“Catalyst”) is a SEBI Registered Cat-I Merchant Banking firm based in Mumbai with presence across India through several well-established associate relations. Catalyst aims to be best in the advisory services on the back of principles of honesty, integrity, discretion, ethics and uncompromising quality.

Based on such principles, Catalyst has progressed immensely and holds a reputed position today. In this era of expansion, we have been able to usher our clients with our modern regime of advisory and financial services. We have wide and deep associations with intermediaries of Capital & Debt Market in India and overseas to raise funds.

Our highly experienced team acts as a one – stop financial and management advisory concern providing the whole gamut of corporate advisory services.

Our focus is on integrated capital market advisory and financial services to Corporate as well as HNI Clients. Catalyst as a trusted corporate advisor, strives to achieve excellence through its core strategy of specialization and diversification across a range of financial services like: Advisory in IPO, Mergers & Acquisitions, Debt Syndications, Takeovers, Cross Border Structures, Tax Management, Strategic & General Corporate Advice, Business Modelling, etc.

Catalyst has strong research capabilities and relationships, which it leverages to close transactions for its clients in stipulated time frames. The clients often require additional financial and consulting services before and after major transactions and during special situations. To satisfy these needs, Catalyst has built a team of people who have the experience and relationships in different sectors to get superlative results. At Catalyst, what our clients find best and advantageous is our warehouse of knowledge, personalized services, cost effectiveness, independent thinking and a willingness to innovate.

We aim to provide solutions to our clients with a vision to maximize their growth by placing the highest importance on quality, professionalism, integrity and confidentiality with management team of the highest calibre.
